About Me

Ever since writing my first program in C and manipulating it to produce a desired output, I have been obsessed with the idea of using software to solve practical problems. Since then, designing and developing the software has been my passion. Data Analytics and Full Stack development calls to all my passions; it incorporates creativity, Analyzation and problem solving.

This passion carried me to pursue my master's in computing at University of Central Lancashire. Here I studied new languages, algorithms, compilers, higher mathematics, all with pretty much the same fascination that drove me as a kid. And it was here that I learned truly how much there was yet to learn. This is what keeps me up at night, a never-ending thirst to create beautiful, powerful things and share them with the world.

So, now, in industry, this passion remains with me. There’s certainly yet more to learn, more problems to solve, and more to build. And for this, I am very grateful. With 2+ years of experience, I am a passionate and diligent student with an understanding of diverse languages, development tools and web technologies. I have started learning AngularJs and enthusiastically grabbing onto any other programming languages, frameworks and principles that I can integrate into the coding web in my head.
